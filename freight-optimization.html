Prototype boundary — fictional, modeled data.

All orders, rates, and savings are simulated and MODELED (published-base-rate estimates), not verified freight invoices. No live ERP, TMS, carrier, or order-management system is contacted, and no customer email or shipping change is sent. Uploaded CSVs are parsed in your browser only.

i
Scan every order; do not create a ticket for every order.

Freight cost anomalies

Unsupervised statistical screening (z-score vs peer groups) that flags freight figures that look unusually high compared to order value or margin.

i
Screening aid, not a shipping decision

This flags published base-rate freight figures that are statistically unusual within Domestic or Export peer groups (or fixed screening thresholds when too few candidates are quoted). It is not a final freight quote, verified savings amount, or shipping decision.
